Work Experience

As a non-traditional student with a years-long array of work experience, I am finding fulfillment and enjoyment in my pursuit of a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design at Indiana University's Eskenazi School of Art, Architecture, and Design. In September 2020, I launched my freelance Graphic Design business, Rhizome Design Co, and look forward to offering my design skills to members of my community.
